Is there a speed limit?
ReFvv allows up to 125 km/hr. However it is 80 km/hr in a few cities where some cars might be equipped with speed governors as per government directives. Revv strictly advises to follow local speed limits.
Can I extend/ cancel/ modify?
Yes, extensions are possible subject to availability & charges. Cancellations & modifications will attract nominal charges as per our policy.
Booking criteria & documents?
Min. 21 years old, have valid original government ID (Aadhar, Passport, or PAN only) and a valid driving licence for “Light Motor Vehicles”, which is min. 1 year old at the time of starting the trip.
Are there any restricted areas?
Leh/Ladhakh, Spiti Valley & Kaza/Nako regions are not permitted to take Revv cars. Customer will be fully liable for any damages incurred to the car in that region.

The historic city of Hyderabad is slowly but surely turning into a bustling tech hub. Engineers from across India are making their way to the City of the Nizams in search of employment and other opportunities. Given the growing demographic of the city, it shouldn’t come as a surprise that many people are looking for car rental services in Hyderabad.

Commuting Within the City

For locals, travelling from residential areas like Begumpet and Kukatpally to the commercial centre of HITEC City can be quite problematic. There are taxis/cabs and buses that ply along the route, but they’re either too expensive or aren’t very frequent. This is why self-drive cars in Hyderabad are an absolute necessity. With a self-drive car, you can travel on your own time and at your own pace.
